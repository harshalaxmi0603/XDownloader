Privacy Policy

Protecting your data and privacy is our top priority.

1. Information We Collect (Minimalism)

XDownloader is designed to be a privacy-first service. We are committed to collecting the absolute minimum amount of information required for our service to function.

  • No Personal Data: We do not require or collect personal identifying information (like your name, email address, or social media login details) to use our download tools.
  • Usage Data (URLs): The video URLs you submit are processed temporarily on our server to fetch and convert the media. These URLs are **not stored** beyond the time necessary to complete the download.
  • Log Data: Like most websites, we collect standard log data (IP address, browser type, timestamps) for security, maintenance, and basic traffic analysis.

2. Data Storage and Deletion

The media files (videos, audio) processed on our servers are temporary. They are automatically **deleted from our servers minutes after the download link is provided** or the session expires. We do not maintain an archive or copy of the downloaded media.

3. Cookies and Tracking

We may use essential cookies to maintain the functionality of the site (e.g., session management). We do not use third-party tracking cookies or sophisticated advertising trackers.

4. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
